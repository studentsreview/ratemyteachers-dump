High Point Regional High School is located in Sussex, NJ. Students and parents can contact High Point Regional High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (973) 875-3101. High Point Regional High School has 1301 total students, 650 male, 651 female. Of these students from High Point Regional High School, 305 are ninth grade students, 279 are tenth grade students, 337 are eleventh grade students, and 328 are twelfth grade students. High Point Regional High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
