Brookville High School is located in Brookville, OH. Students and parents can contact Brookville High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (937) 833-6761. Brookville High School has 531 total students, 265 male, 262 female. Of these students from Brookville High School, 141 are ninth grade students, 121 are tenth grade students, 124 are eleventh grade students, and 145 are twelfth grade students. Brookville High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 23:1.
