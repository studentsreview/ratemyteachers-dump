Centennial High School is located in Roswell, GA. Students and parents can contact Centennial High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (770) 650-4230. Centennial High School has 2043 total students, 1040 male, 941 female. Of these students from Centennial High School, 591 are ninth grade students, 495 are tenth grade students, 524 are eleventh grade students, and 433 are twelfth grade students. Centennial High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 15:1.
