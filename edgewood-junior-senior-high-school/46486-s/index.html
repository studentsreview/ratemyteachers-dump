Edgewood Junior / Senior High School is located in Merritt Island, FL. Students and parents can contact Edgewood Junior / Senior High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (321) 454-1030. Edgewood Junior / Senior High School has 946 total students, 421 male, 497 female.
