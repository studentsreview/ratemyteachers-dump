Houston County High School is located in Warner Robins, GA. Students and parents can contact Houston County High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (478) 988-6340. Houston County High School has 2231 total students, 1158 male, 1036 female. Of these students from Houston County High School, 696 are ninth grade students, 561 are tenth grade students, 544 are eleventh grade students, and 430 are twelfth grade students. Houston County High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
