Bellevue High School is located in Bellevue, WA. Students and parents can contact Bellevue High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (425) 456-7002. Bellevue High School has 1361 total students, 703 male, 592 female. Of these students from Bellevue High School, 305 are ninth grade students, 345 are tenth grade students, 363 are eleventh grade students, and 348 are twelfth grade students. Bellevue High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
