La Salle High School is located in Miami, FL. Students and parents can contact La Salle High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (754) 323-0900. La Salle High School has 1498 total students, 694 male, 783 female. Of these students from La Salle High School, 468 are ninth grade students, 405 are tenth grade students, 335 are eleventh grade students, and 290 are twelfth grade students. La Salle High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
