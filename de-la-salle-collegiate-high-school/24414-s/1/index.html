De La Salle Collegiate High School is located in Warren, MI. Students and parents can contact De La Salle Collegiate High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (906) 643-8800. De La Salle Collegiate High School has 309 total students, 164 male, 144 female. Of these students from De La Salle Collegiate High School, 62 are ninth grade students, 60 are tenth grade students, 65 are eleventh grade students, and 65 are twelfth grade students. De La Salle Collegiate High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
