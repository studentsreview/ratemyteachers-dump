Jonathan Law High School is located in Milford, CT. Students and parents can contact Jonathan Law High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (203) 783-3574. Jonathan Law High School has 1034 total students, 517 male, 517 female. Of these students from Jonathan Law High School, 244 are ninth grade students, 267 are tenth grade students, 261 are eleventh grade students, and 262 are twelfth grade students.
