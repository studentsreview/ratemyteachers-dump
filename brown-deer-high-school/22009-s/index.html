Brown Deer High School is located in Brown Deer, WI. Students and parents can contact Brown Deer High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (414) 371-7000. Brown Deer High School has 680 total students, 376 male, 304 female. Of these students from Brown Deer High School, 173 are ninth grade students, 172 are tenth grade students, 179 are eleventh grade students, and 156 are twelfth grade students. Brown Deer High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 16:1.
