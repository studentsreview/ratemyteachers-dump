Cypress Bay High School is located in Weston, FL. Students and parents can contact Cypress Bay High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (754) 323-0350. Cypress Bay High School has 5492 total students, 2772 male, 2605 female. Of these students from Cypress Bay High School, 1412 are ninth grade students, 1441 are tenth grade students, 1382 are eleventh grade students, and 1257 are twelfth grade students. Cypress Bay High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 20:1.
