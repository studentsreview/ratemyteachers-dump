Godwin High School is located in Richmond, VA. Students and parents can contact Godwin High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (804) 750-2600. Godwin High School has 1878 total students, 887 male, 896 female. Of these students from Godwin High School, 516 are ninth grade students, 429 are tenth grade students, 438 are eleventh grade students, and 495 are twelfth grade students. Godwin High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
