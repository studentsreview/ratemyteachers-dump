Facebook Share

Florida Virtual High School is located in Orlando, FL. Students and parents can contact Florida Virtual High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (407) 513-3587. Florida Virtual High School has 12730 total students, 4837 male, 7057 female. Of these students from Florida Virtual High School, 1208 are ninth grade students, 3184 are tenth grade students, 3873 are eleventh grade students, and 4465 are twelfth grade students. Florida Virtual High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 30:1.
