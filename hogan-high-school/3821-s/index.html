Hogan High School is located in Vallejo, CA. Students and parents can contact Hogan High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (707) 556-3510. Hogan High School has 1538 total students, 777 male, 761 female. Of these students from Hogan High School, 457 are ninth grade students, 486 are tenth grade students, 304 are eleventh grade students, and 290 are twelfth grade students. Hogan High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
