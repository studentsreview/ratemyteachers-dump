Gaither High School is located in Tampa, FL. Students and parents can contact Gaither High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (813) 975-7340. Gaither High School has 2360 total students, 1117 male, 1148 female. Of these students from Gaither High School, 708 are ninth grade students, 597 are tenth grade students, 531 are eleventh grade students, and 523 are twelfth grade students. Gaither High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 19:1.
