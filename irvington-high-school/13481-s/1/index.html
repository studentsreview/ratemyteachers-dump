Irvington High School is located in Irvington, NY. Students and parents can contact Irvington High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (914) 591-8648. Irvington High School has 621 total students, 304 male, 316 female. Of these students from Irvington High School, 163 are ninth grade students, 140 are tenth grade students, 155 are eleventh grade students, and 163 are twelfth grade students. Irvington High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 13:1.
