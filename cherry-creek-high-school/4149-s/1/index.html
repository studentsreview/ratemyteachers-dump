Cherry Creek High School is located in Englewood, CO. Students and parents can contact Cherry Creek High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (720) 554-2000. Cherry Creek High School has 3719 total students, 1931 male, 1788 female. Of these students from Cherry Creek High School, 855 are ninth grade students, 934 are tenth grade students, 954 are eleventh grade students, and 976 are twelfth grade students.
