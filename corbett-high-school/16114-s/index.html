Corbett High School is located in Corbett, OR. Students and parents can contact Corbett High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (503) 695-3600. Corbett High School has 696 total students, 336 male, 355 female. Of these students from Corbett High School, 63 are ninth grade students, 80 are tenth grade students, 60 are eleventh grade students, and 31 are twelfth grade students. Corbett High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 21:1.
