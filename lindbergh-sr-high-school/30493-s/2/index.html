Lindbergh Sr. High School is located in St. Louis, MO. Students and parents can contact Lindbergh Sr. High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (314) 729-2410. Lindbergh Sr. High School has 1946 total students, 984 male, 962 female. Of these students from Lindbergh Sr. High School, 488 are ninth grade students, 480 are tenth grade students, 472 are eleventh grade students, and 506 are twelfth grade students. Lindbergh Sr. High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 17:1.
