Cheboygan Area High School is located in Cheboygan, MI. Students and parents can contact Cheboygan Area High School by writing to the above mailing address or by calling (231) 627-7191. Cheboygan Area High School has 789 total students, 397 male, 371 female. Of these students from Cheboygan Area High School, 239 are ninth grade students, 194 are tenth grade students, 182 are eleventh grade students, and 152 are twelfth grade students. Cheboygan Area High School maintains a student to teacher ratio of 18:1.
